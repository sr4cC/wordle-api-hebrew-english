# wordle-api---hebrew-and-english
takes a word and chooses it for the daily word (in english and hebrew) has some commands look in README


/is-exists-english
checks if the word exists inside the english word list

/is-exists-hebrew
checks if the word exists inside the hebrew word list

/random
gets a random word out of the english word list

/random-hebrew
gets a random word out of the hebrew word list

/today
gets the word that was chosen from the english word list at 00:00

/today-hebrew
gets the word that was chosen from the hebrew word list at 00:00

/stats
gives the 10 last day words (english only) how much words were used from the english word list
